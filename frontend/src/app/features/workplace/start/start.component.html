Add more details about your workplace

We'll ask you a maximum of 16 questions. If necessary, you can skip questions and answer them later in the Workplace section of ASC-WDS.

We would like to know:

  • whether you provide any other services?

  • the capacity of the services that you provide (if applicable)?

  • who uses your services?

  • whether you have any current staff vacancies?

  • whether you've had new starters or leavers in the last 12 months?

  • how much money is being spent on advertising for staff?

  • how many people are being interviewed for care worker roles?

  • whether new care workers have to repeat training done with previous employers?

  • whether Care Certificates are transferable from workplace to workplace?

  • whether a cash loyalty bonus is paid within the first 2 years of employment?

  • whether care workers are paid more than Statutory Sick Pay?

  • whether more than 3% is contributed towards care worker pensions?

  • how many days holiday leave full-time care workers get?

  • whether we can share your data?
