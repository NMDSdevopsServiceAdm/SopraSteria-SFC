Start to add your staff records

To add a staff record

You need to know the staff member's:

  • name or ID number
  • main job role
  • contract type

After you save this mandatory information you can continue to add more details, so
that you get a complete record for that staff member.

You'll be asked about that staff member's:

  • personal details, like their date of birth and National Insurance number
  • employment details, like when they started their main job role
  • qualifications and training
