Learning provider offers – QACLS assured courses and qualifications

Skills for Care helps deliver the Quality Assured Care Learning Service, which reviews training provider and course quality, making sure training is accessible, high-quality, and meets the needs of both the workforce and people who draw on care and support. More information about the Quality Assured Care Learning Service is available here (opens in a new window).

On this page you will find additional offers that certain providers have agreed to specifically make available to you as part of your registered manager membership – this is in addition to the provision available for these courses from the Learning and Development Support Scheme(opens in a new window) for the adult social care workforce.
