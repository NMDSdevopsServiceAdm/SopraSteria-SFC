What's new in ASC-WDS?

We've added NEW functionality that lets you reuse training courses details in the training records you add.

You'll only enter the details of a course once, so this should save you time and make your training records more accurate.

To add the details of a training course:

  • Go to training and qualifications.
  • Click 'Add and manage training' button.
  • Select the 'Add training courses' option.
  • Click the 'Add a training course' button and add the details.

You'll then be able to include the details of the training course in any training record that you add or update in the future.
