What's new in ASC-WDS?

We've added NEW functionality that lets you create a list of training courses that people in your workplace do.

Then, when you add a training record, you can select a course and the details of that course will be automatically populated for you (for example, the training course name and expiry date).

This will save you time and make your training records more accurate.

To add a training course to the list:

  • Go to training and qualifications.
  • Click the 'Add and manage training' button.
  • Select the 'Add and manage training courses' option.
  • Click the 'Add a training course' button and add the course details.
