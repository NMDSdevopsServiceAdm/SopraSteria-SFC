Bulk uploads let you add large amounts of workforce data into the Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set, without the need to add that data manually.

Skills for Care recommend bulk uploads for local authorities and large private or voluntary employers who need to add data for subsidiary workplaces.

Individual workplaces with fewer than 50 staff members often find it easier to add their data manually.

Download your current data and update the records in those CSV files before you do the bulk upload.

You can download codes and guidance to use as references for how to update the records in your files.
