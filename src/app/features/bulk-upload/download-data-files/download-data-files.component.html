You must use the codes to fill in your data in the files. The codes represent information you can enter or edit about your staff and workplace.

Download your files and the latest version of the codes. You can then fill in your files by entering the correct code into each field. The field headings are listed next to the relevant codes.

For example To add the contract type 'Permanent' to a staff record you can enter '1' for that row under the 'EMPLSTATUS' heading.
