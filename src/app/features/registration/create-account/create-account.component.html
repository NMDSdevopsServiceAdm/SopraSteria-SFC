Create an Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set account

Adult social care providers and local authorities should use this service to create an Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set (ASC-WDS) account.

Skills for Care will check your account and let you know when your details have been validated, either by email or telephone.

Once your account has been validated, you'll be able to manage your workplace information, staff records, training and qualifications all in one place.

Before you start

You can read about the benefits of having an ASC-WDS account on the Skills for Care website or view our 'about ASC-WDS' page.

To create an account you need to know some basic details about your workplace (including your CQC location ID if you have one) and the main care service you provide.

You will not be able to sign in until your account has been validated by Skills for Care.
