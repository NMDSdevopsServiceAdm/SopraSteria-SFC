About the data

Who uses the data

The Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC) use the data collected by this service.

The data helps DHSC, the Care Quality Commission and local authorities make decisions about the adult social care sector.

Benchmarks

The amount and quality of the data you add will make the benchmarks more accurate and useful.

Staff records are not included in benchmark calculations when there’s missing data. For example, if you have 4 staff records and only 3 contain pay data, only those 3 records would be included in the benchmark for pay.

Comparison group

Your comparison group data is refreshed every month and is only included after it passes extensive quality checks.

The size of the comparison group can change depending on which group you select, the number of workplaces and the amount of data provided.

Not enough data

Benchmarks will not show if:

  • there are not enough workplaces or staff in a comparison group

  • you’ve not added any data about something (for example, pay or a particular job role)

Care worker and senior care worker pay

Your workplace data for care worker pay and senior care worker pay will not show if they’re saved as annual salaries.

Registered manager and registered nurse salaries

Your workplace data for these salaries will not show if they’re saved as hourly rates of pay.

Vacancy rate

Your workplace vacancy rate will not show until you’ve added vacancy information and at least the mandatory staff record information needed for each staff member included in your total number of staff.

Turnover rate

Your workplace turnover rate will not show until you’ve added leaver information and at least the mandatory staff record information needed for each staff member included in your total number of staff.

Percentage of staff still in their main Job role after 12 months

Your workplace percentage of staff will not show unless you’ve added a staff record for each staff member included in your total number of staff. You’ll also need to have added a main job role start date for each staff member.

Data protection

Skills for Care is committed to protecting your privacy and security. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation ((EU) 2016/679) and the Data Protection Act 2018.
